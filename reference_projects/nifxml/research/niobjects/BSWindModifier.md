# Niobject `BSWindModifier`

Particle Modifier that uses the wind value from the gamedata to alter the path of particles.

## Attributes
- **inherit**: `NiPSysModifier`
- **module**: `BSParticle`
- **name**: `BSWindModifier`
- **versions**: `#BETHESDA#`

## Fields
- **Strength** (`float`)
  - Attributes: `default`=`0.25`
  - The amount of force wind will have on particles.

# Niobject `NiRangeLODData`

NiRangeLODData controls switching LOD levels based on Z depth from the camera to the NiLODNode.

- **inherit**: `NiLODData`
- **module**: `NiMain`
- **name**: `NiRangeLODData`

## Fields
- **LOD Center** (`Vector3`)
- **Num LOD Levels** (`uint`)
- **LOD Levels** (`LODRange`)
  - Attributes: `length`=`Num LOD Levels`

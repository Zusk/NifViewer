# Niobject `NiGravity`

LEGACY (pre-10.1) particle modifier. Applies a gravitational field on the particles.

## Attributes
- **inherit**: `NiParticleModifier`
- **module**: `NiLegacy`
- **name**: `NiGravity`
- **until**: `V10_0_1_0`

## Fields
- **Decay** (`float`)
  - Attributes: `since`=`3.3.0.13`
- **Force** (`float`)
  - The strength/force of this gravity.
- **Type** (`FieldType`)
  - The force field type.
- **Position** (`Vector3`)
  - The position of the mass point relative to the particle system.
- **Direction** (`Vector3`)
  - The direction of the applied acceleration.
- **Unknown 01** (`float`)
  - Attributes: `until`=`2.3`
- **Unknown 02** (`float`)
  - Attributes: `until`=`2.3`

# Enum `ImageType`

Determines how the raw image data is stored in NiRawImageData.

## Attributes
- **module**: `NiMain`
- **name**: `ImageType`
- **storage**: `uint`

## Values
- `option` `RGB` (`value`=`1`) – Colors store red, blue, and green components.
- `option` `RGBA` (`value`=`2`) – Colors store red, blue, green, and alpha components.

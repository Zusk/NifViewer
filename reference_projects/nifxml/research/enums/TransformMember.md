# Enum `TransformMember`

Describes which aspect of the NiTextureTransform the NiTextureTransformController will modify.

## Attributes
- **name**: `TransformMember`
- **storage**: `uint`

## Values
- `option` `TT_TRANSLATE_U` (`value`=`0`) – Control the translation of the U coordinates.
- `option` `TT_TRANSLATE_V` (`value`=`1`) – Control the translation of the V coordinates.
- `option` `TT_ROTATE` (`value`=`2`) – Control the rotation of the coordinates.
- `option` `TT_SCALE_U` (`value`=`3`) – Control the scale of the U coordinates.
- `option` `TT_SCALE_V` (`value`=`4`) – Control the scale of the V coordinates.

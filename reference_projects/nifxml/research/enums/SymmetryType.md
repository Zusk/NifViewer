# Enum `SymmetryType`

Describes the symmetry type of bomb forces.

## Attributes
- **name**: `SymmetryType`
- **storage**: `uint`

## Values
- `option` `SPHERICAL_SYMMETRY` (`value`=`0`) – Spherical Symmetry.
- `option` `CYLINDRICAL_SYMMETRY` (`value`=`1`) – Cylindrical Symmetry.
- `option` `PLANAR_SYMMETRY` (`value`=`2`) – Planar Symmetry.

# Enum `hkResponseType`

hkpMaterial::ResponseType

## Attributes
- **name**: `hkResponseType`
- **storage**: `byte`
- **versions**: `#BETHESDA#`

## Values
- `option` `RESPONSE_INVALID` (`value`=`0`) – Invalid Response
- `option` `RESPONSE_SIMPLE_CONTACT` (`value`=`1`) – Do normal collision resolution
- `option` `RESPONSE_REPORTING` (`value`=`2`) – No collision resolution is performed but listeners are called
- `option` `RESPONSE_NONE` (`value`=`3`) – Do nothing, ignore all the results.

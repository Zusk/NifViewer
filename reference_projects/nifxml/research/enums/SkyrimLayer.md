# Enum `SkyrimLayer`

Bethesda Havok. Describes the collision layer a body belongs to in Skyrim.

## Attributes
- **name**: `SkyrimLayer`
- **storage**: `byte`
- **versions**: `#SKY_AND_LATER#`

# Enum `VelocityType`

Controls the way the a particle mesh emitter determines the starting speed and direction of the particles that are emitted.

## Attributes
- **name**: `VelocityType`
- **storage**: `uint`

## Values
- `option` `VELOCITY_USE_NORMALS` (`value`=`0`) – Uses the normals of the meshes to determine staring velocity.
- `option` `VELOCITY_USE_RANDOM` (`value`=`1`) – Starts particles with a random velocity.
- `option` `VELOCITY_USE_DIRECTION` (`value`=`2`) – Uses the emission axis to determine initial particle direction?

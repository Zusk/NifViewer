# Enum `Fallout3Layer`

Bethesda Havok. Describes the collision layer a body belongs to in Fallout 3 and Fallout NV.

## Attributes
- **name**: `Fallout3Layer`
- **storage**: `byte`
- **versions**: `#BETHESDA#`

# Enum `EndianType`

## Attributes
- **name**: `EndianType`
- **storage**: `byte`

## Values
- `option` `ENDIAN_BIG` (`value`=`0`) – The numbers are stored in big endian format, such as those used by PowerPC Mac processors.
- `option` `ENDIAN_LITTLE` (`value`=`1`) – The numbers are stored in little endian format, such as those used by Intel and AMD x86 processors.

# Bitflags `AccumFlags`

Describes the options for the accum root on NiControllerSequence.

## Attributes
- **name**: `AccumFlags`
- **storage**: `uint`

## Values
- `option` `ACCUM_X_TRANS` (`bit`=`0`) – X Translation will be accumulated.
- `option` `ACCUM_Y_TRANS` (`bit`=`1`) – Y Translation will be accumulated.
- `option` `ACCUM_Z_TRANS` (`bit`=`2`) – Z Translation will be accumulated.
- `option` `ACCUM_X_ROT` (`bit`=`3`) – X Rotation will be accumulated.
- `option` `ACCUM_Y_ROT` (`bit`=`4`) – Y Rotation will be accumulated.
- `option` `ACCUM_Z_ROT` (`bit`=`5`) – Z Rotation will be accumulated.
- `option` `ACCUM_X_FRONT` (`bit`=`6`) – +X is front facing. (Default)
- `option` `ACCUM_Y_FRONT` (`bit`=`7`) – +Y is front facing.
- `option` `ACCUM_Z_FRONT` (`bit`=`8`) – +Z is front facing.
- `option` `ACCUM_NEG_FRONT` (`bit`=`9`) – -X is front facing.

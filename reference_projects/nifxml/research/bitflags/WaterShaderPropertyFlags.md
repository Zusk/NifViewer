# Bitflags `WaterShaderPropertyFlags`

Skyrim water shader property flags

## Attributes
- **name**: `WaterShaderPropertyFlags`
- **prefix**: `BSWSP`
- **storage**: `uint`
- **versions**: `#SKY_AND_LATER#`
